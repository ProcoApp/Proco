Proco

A mobile app that helps you make everyday decisions by posting your choices to a social platform where people give their opinions and votes using just a swipe.



Sign up for Proco updates. We will never spam you.

Post

Multiple Options


Users can ask their questions and polls using a simple interface. Creating a poll is easy: users simply creates an account or login through facebook, type their question, provide upto four choices with an optional image, select the category of people they want to post it to. That's it. Social integration through facebook allows you to share the question with just your friends and location service allows you to share it with people near you. If the user don't have an image for an option, no problem, Proco will pull an image from the web making your poll attractive.



Statistics


Get an in-depth view of the answers to your question on a dedicated statistics page. The page will show the number of votes, the overall distribution of votes and the distribution of your friend's votes.



Vote

Tap and Swipe


Put your wisdom to good use. Poll topics are limitless: from fashion, to food, to healthcare and more. People can express their opinions easily with just a tap. You don't even have to jump through different pages to see how everyone else voted, to follow a question or start a discussion. Just tap again to see the overall responses, follow or comment. Otherwise just swipe to see the next question.



Karma Score


Additionally, voting or commenting on a poll awards you with karma points. As the karma score increases, your reputation in the community increases and your votes become more credible. If your choice is the final decision after the poll is closed then you get bonus karma.



Filters

Friends, Followers and More


Don't want to see public questions? You can filter the polls that you answer to just be from your friends. You can also follow people you find interesting and choose to see their questions.If you want, just answer the questions of people near you. Otherwise, you can answer all the polls open to the general public from all around the world.



Filtered Polls


Proco also decides which questions appears to the user. It predicts how interesting each question will be to each user. If it finds that a particular user will find this category of questions boring then it hides them.
